Why Cyber Awareness Matters

Children use the internet for:

  • Online classes & Research
  • Games & Entertainment
  • Social media & Chatting

Potential Risks:

  • Financial scams
  • Cyberbullying
  • Privacy & Data leaks

"Cyber awareness helps children stay safe, curious, and confident online."

How Parents Can Support

Open Dialogue

Talk openly about internet use. Make them feel safe to report anything strange.

Teach Safety

Teach safety, not fear. Explain the 'why' behind the rules.

Set Limits

Set healthy screen-time limits to encourage balance.

Ask Questions

Encourage children to ask questions before clicking or sharing.

üîê

Important Safety Tips

  • No Password Sharing: Teach children not to share passwords with anyone (except you).
  • Link Caution: Explain the dangers of clicking unknown links or ads.
  • Privacy First: Guide them on setting social media profiles to 'Private'.
  • App Monitoring: Stay aware of the apps and games they use regularly.

Signs a Child May Need Help

  • Sudden fear or stress after using the internet
  • Extreme secrecy about online activity
  • Receiving strange messages or items from unknown contacts
